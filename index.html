A few things to know about VoteWise

  • Independent and unofficial — not affiliated with any political party, electoral commission, or government body.
  • Built with AI assistance — policy positions may not capture every nuance, always read official manifestos before you vote.
  • A guide only — voting is personal, this tool helps you explore policies, not tells you how to vote.
  • Results are indicative only, based on alignment with known party positions.
  • Available in English only — we apologise to those who would prefer other languages.
  • No personal data collected — your answers never leave your browser.
  • Covers main registered parties only — independents and smaller candidates may not be included.
  • VoteWise works across multiple elections — always check you are viewing the correct election.
